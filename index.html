About

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Peking University, School of Computer Science, working with Prof. Xiang Chen in the IFLab. I received my B.Eng. degree in Software Engineering from the School of Information and Software Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, in 2025.

Recently, I have been working on efficient video generation, with a focus on sparse attention and real-time generation for video generation models. If you are interested in discussing related topics, feel free to send me an email.
